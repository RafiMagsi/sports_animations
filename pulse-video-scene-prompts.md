# PULSE — Background Video Scene Prompts (for Grok Imagine)

Generic, unbranded football content — no real players/teams referenced, so nothing to clear rights on. Each prompt is written as one paste-able block. Swap the bracketed bits `[like this]` to retune color/mood without rewriting the whole prompt — everything here defaults to the site's palette (near-black background, cyan/violet/pink energy accents) so clips cut together cleanly.

Keep every clip **9:16 or 16:9, static or slow push-in camera, 4–8 seconds** — the site scrubs these frame-by-frame on scroll, so a locked-off or slow, steady camera reads far better than handheld shake or fast cuts baked into the footage itself.

### Notes for generating
- If Grok Imagine supports negative prompts, exclude: *text overlays, logos, watermarks, multiple people in frame, visible faces (for the "alone" set), shaky handheld camera, fast cuts*.
- For continuity across clips, keep restating the same palette words (**cyan / violet / pink**, near-black background) — that's what makes 20 separately-generated clips feel like one coherent video rather than a mismatched compilation.
- Generate a couple of takes per prompt — high-speed/slow-motion AI video often produces one dud stretch per clip (a warped hand, an odd frame), and having options to pick the cleanest few seconds from matters more than it sounds.
